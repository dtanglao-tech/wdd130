Family Scenic Float

A relaxing and scenic rafting experience perfect for families and beginners. Enjoy calm waters, gentle rapids, and breathtaking canyon views guided by our certified professionals.

Equipment Included:

  • Raft and paddles
  • Life jackets (PFDs)
  • Helmet
  • Safety briefing and guide

Duration: 3 Hours

Difficulty: Class I-II

River Rapids Adventure

Experience thrilling Class III rapids exciting drops and fast-moving currents. Perfect for adventurous beginners and intermediate rafters seeking more excitement.

Equipment Included:

  • High-performance raft
  • Paddles and helmets
  • Life jackets
  • Wet suits (if needed)
  • Professional river guide

Duration: 5 Hours

Difficulty: Class III

Extreme White Water Expedition

Our most intense and adrenaline-packed adventure featuring powerful rapids, steep drops, and technical river navigation. Designed for experienced rafters looking for the ultimate challenge.

Equipment Included:

  • Expedition-grade raft
  • Advanced safety gear
  • Helmet and reinforced life vest
  • Dry bags for personal items
  • Full-day certified guide team

Duration: Full Day

Difficulty: Class IV-V
